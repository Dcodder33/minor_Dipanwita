Frequently Asked Questions - Donations

How can I donate to the NGO? ▼

You can donate directly through our website using the "Donate Now" button, or you can transfer funds to our official bank account. All details are available in the "Contact Us" section.

What types of donations do you accept? ▼

We accept monetary donations as well as material donations such as food, clothes, books, and medical supplies. Please contact us for specific needs.

Are donations tax-deductible? ▼

Yes, donations made to our NGO are eligible for tax benefits under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. A receipt will be provided for all donations.

How are the donations utilized? ▼

Donations are utilized to fund our projects such as education for underprivileged children, food distribution, and healthcare programs. Regular updates and reports are shared with our donors.

Can I donate anonymously? ▼

Yes, you can donate anonymously. Simply check the "Anonymous" option when making a donation through our online platform.

How can I track the impact of my donation? ▼

We send regular updates to our donors about how their contributions are making a difference. You can also subscribe to our newsletter for detailed reports.

Your support helps us create a brighter future for those in need.
